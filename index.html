Born some 50 years ago. First in a family of four children. Holds a doctorate degree in Educational Psychology from the University of Lagos. She is an Emotional Intelligence hearing expert. A Consultant and Trainer. She facilitates for National Open University of Nigeria (N.O.U.N). Dr.(Mrs) Uchenna Azuka-Obieke is also deeply involved in Children and Teenager Ministries. She resides in Lagos with her husband and three lovely children. She often regarded as "Mummy Uche" because of her love and magnimity shown towards people especially children.
